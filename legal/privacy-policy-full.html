Privacy Policy for Chess Arena

Effective Date: July 25, 2025

Chess Arena (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile app and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you register through Pi Network or other login methods, we may collect basic profile information such as your Pi username, email, or device ID (if allowed by the platform).

Usage Data: We collect data on app usage, puzzle completion, match statistics, leaderboard participation, and social interactions.

Device Data: We may collect information such as your mobile device model, OS version, and app version to improve app performance and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Chess Arena profile
  • To deliver game features (puzzles, matches, leaderboards)
  • To personalize your experience and improve the app
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To communicate updates, promotions, or service changes

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Third-party analytics tools (e.g., Firebase) for performance insights

  • Law enforcement if required to comply with legal obligations

4. Your Privacy Rights

You may access, update, or delete your account through the app settings or request data removal by contacting us at chessarena.legal@proton.me.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee 100% security due to the nature of the internet and mobile networks.

6. Children's Privacy

Chess Arena is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have, we will delete the data promptly.
