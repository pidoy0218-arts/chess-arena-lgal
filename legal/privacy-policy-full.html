Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 6, 2025

Welcome to The Chess Arena Pi. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: If you log in using the Pi Network, we may collect your Pi username, wallet ID (for rewards), and profile photo.
  • Game Data: We store your gameplay activity, win/loss history, and leaderboard ranking.
  • Device Info: We may collect basic device information (browser, screen size) to improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display rankings and match results.
  • To provide Pi-based rewards in the future.
  • To enhance app performance and detect bugs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data:

  • With Pi Network, for login and verification purposes.
  • As required by law, if legally obligated.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure storage practices to keep your data safe. However, no method is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
  • You may revoke access by logging out of Pi Network.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. We will notify users via the app or Pi Browser if significant changes occur.

7. Contact Us
